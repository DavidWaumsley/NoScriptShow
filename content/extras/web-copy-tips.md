<h1 class="post-title-only">Web Copy Tips</h1>

## Write for Scanners, Not Readers

- Place the most critical information at the top.
- Use short sentences, brief paragraphs, bullet points, and bolded text to make content digestible.
- **Headlines are King:** aim for descriptive, benefit-driven headlines.
- Think "mobile-first".

## Focus on Benefits Over Features
- Ask "So what?" to turn it into a benefit.

## Clear is better than clever
Avoid jargon or being overly creative at the expense of clarity.

- Use simple language to make your message accessible to everyone.
- Write like you speak, addressing the reader directly.

## Build Trust (Social Proof)

- Quantify rather than claiming to be the "best". 

## Consider keywords, but don’t keyword stuff

 - HTML &lt;details&gt; and &lt;summary&gt; elements provide a way to add extra text without overwhelming the page.
 - Typically if we half the copy it has more clarity.
